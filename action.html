Action Movies Reviews

Action is a movie genre in which the protagonists must overcome several challenges that usually involves physical feats, protracted combat scenes, bloodshed, and fast-paced chases. Action movies often features a resourceful character overcoming enormous challenges such as life-threatening circumstances, villains on the pursuit that usually results in the hero's victory. Action genre started to emerge in the 1970s alongside the rise of stunts and special effects. This genre is extremely related to thriller and adventure genres and might include aspects of espionage and spy fiction.
